Zerudanodensetsu shirīzu (zeruda no den setsu shirīzu, Ei: The Legend of Zelda series) wa Nintendō ga kaihatsu hatsubai shite iru konpyūtagēmushirīzu [6 ]. Gaiyō 1986-nen no shodai “zerudanodensetsu” no hatsubai irai, nagaki ni watatte yūzā ni shiji sa rete iru, Nintendō no akushon'adobenchāgēmu [7 ], matawa akushon'ārupījī [chū 1]. Shodai “zerudanodensetsu” wa miyamoto shigeru, tedzuka takashi, Nakagō Toshihiko, kondō kōji nado no wazuka sū-ri ni yotte famirīkonpyūta disuku shisutemu no rōnchisofuto to shite kaihatsu sa reta [8 ] [9 ]. Ōku no shirīzu sakuhin no kyōtsū-ten to shite `samazamana danjon o kōryaku'`ken o mein to suru akushon'`aitemu ni yoru seichō'[7 ] `bōken no butai ga hakoniwa' nado ga aru [10]. Kihon-tekina sutōrī wa pureiyā no bunshindearu rinku ga ganon nado no teki kara ohimesama no zeruda o tasukeru to iu monogatari. Sakuhin-goto ni jidai ya shujinkō no settei wa kotonari, sutōrī mo dokuritsu shita mono ni natte iru tame, shirīzu no tochū kara demo rikai dekiru mono ni natte iru [6 ]. Ippō de `hairaru no rekishi' to shōsa reru gēmu-jō no jikeiretsu wa sonzai shi, seisaku-sha e no intabyū ya kanren shojakura de tabitabi genkyū sa rete iru (kōjutsu no `jikeiretsu' no kō mo sanshō) [11]. Gēmushisutemu 2 D zeruda 3 D zeruda zerudanodensetsu shirīzu wa shodai “zerudanodensetsu” “kamigami no toraifōsu” “yume o miru shima” “fushigi no konomi” to 2 D no toppubyū (fukan)-gata no gēmushisutemu o saiyō shite ori,`zerudanodensetsu = toppubyū no gēmu' to iu zushiki ga kakuritsu sa reta [12]. 1998-Nen hatsubai no “tokinookarina” wa kore made no toppubyū-gata no gēmushisutemu kara tenkan shi, nintendō 64 no kinō o ikashi 3 DCG no fīrudo o san'ninshō shiten de sōsa suru 3 D akushongēmu to natta [13] [14]. Kore made no gēmu-sei o sokonawanai rittai hyōgen ni yoru rinbakan ya nazotoki, 3 D kūkan de no sumūzuna ken akushon o jitsugen shita [12] [15]. Kono hoka saidobyū ni yoru yoko sukurōruakushon ( “rinkunobōken”), shūtingugēmu ( “rinku no bougantorēningu” ) nado mo rirīsu sa rete iru [15]. Akushon buki ni yoru kōgeki ya aitemu no shiyō nado hōfuna akushon ga kanō to natte iru. 3 D ni natta “tokinookarina”de wa `Tate kiri'`yoko kiri'`janpugiri' to itta kenjutsu mo tsuika sa re [16],“kazenotakuto” ya “towairaitopurinsesu”de wa taimingu yoku kōgeki suru koto de ōwaza o kuridasu koto ga dekiru [17] [18]. Mata,“sukaiu~ōdosōdo”de wa u~ī rimokon purasu ni yoru sōsa de yori chokkan-tekina akushon ga kanō to natta [19]. Chūmoku shisutemu “tokinookarina” ikō no 3 D sakuhin de tōsai sa rete iru, botan o osu koto de teki ya jinbutsu nado o jidō de rokkuon dekiru shisutemu. “Tokinookarina” to “mujuranokamen” no sai wa, Z torigābotan o osu koto kara `Z chūmoku shisutemu' to yoba reta [14]. Nazotoki aitemu zerudanodensetsu shirīzude wa, kihontekini danjon de nazotoki-yō no aitemu o nyūshu shi, sore o tsukatte danjon o kōryaku shite iku gēmudezain ga nasa rete iru [20]. “Kazenotakuto”de wa rinku no me no ugoki kara nazotoki no hinto o e tari [21],“sukaiu~ōdosōdo”de wa u~ī rimokon purasu o tsukatte aitemu sōsa shi tari [22] suru nado, nazotoki no hōhō wa sakuhin-goto ni mo tokuchō ga aru. “Kamigami no toraifōsu 2”de wa kore made no shirīzu to kotonari, nazotoki-yō no aitemu o mise de kōnyū dekiru yō ni natte iru [23]. Kō gaido mōdo ichibu no sakuhinde wa kō gaido mōdo to shite `ura zeruda'[24] ya `karakuchi mōdo'[25] ga tōsai sa rete iru. `Ura zeruda' wa `omote' to danjon no kōzō ya nazotoki yōso ga mattaku kotonari,`karakuchi mōdo'de wa hi damēji ga 2-bai ni naru nado no kibishī jōken de purei suru koto ni naru. Gurafikku “kazenotakuto” ikō, tokuni 3 D zeruda wa samazamana gurafikku de rirīsu sa rete ori,“kazenotakuto”de wa to~ūnrendaringu o mochiita anime-chō no gurafikku [26],“towairaitopurinsesu”de wa kyarakutā no tōshin ga agatta riaruna gurafikku to nari [15],“sukaiu~ōdosōdo” wa to~ūnrendaringu no gihō o bubun-teki ni shiyō shita hāfuto~ūn to iu gihō de funwari to shita yawarakai gurafikku o jitsugen shite iru [27]. Tokuni “kazenotakuto”de wa `sawareru animēshon' o utai, chirashi nado de daidaiteki ni senden sa rete ita [16]. Ongaku shodai “zerudanodensetsu” no ongaku ya kōka-on wa tōji nyūsha 2-nen-me no kondō kōji ga seisaku o tantō [28]. Igo mo Kondō wa zeruda no ongaku ni tazusawari, seisaku kanshū nado o okonatte iru [29] [30]. Gēmu-nai de tsukawa reru nazotoki no sai no kōka-on to takarabako o aketa sai no kōka-on wa shirīzu o tōshite ōkiku kaeru koto naku shiyō sa re tsudzukete iru [31]. Mata,“towairaitopurinsesu” ikō no sakuhinde wa ōkesutora ongen ga tabitabi mochii rareru yō ni natta [32]. Zerudashirīzu no ongaku wa,`gēmu ongaku'-naradeha no atarashī apurōchi ga nasa reru no mo tokuchōdearu. “Tokinookarina”de wa hairaru heigen no BGM no tenkai ga maikai kawaru [28], teki to tatakau sai ni shīmuresu de sentō-kyoku ni kirikawaru [33],“kazenotakuto”de wa kōgeki hitto-ji ni kyoku no kōdo shinkō ni awaseta onkai no to~utti-on ga naru [30], teki to hikaridama o uchiau sai ni BGM ga han'on zutsu agaru [30] to itta kokoromi ga okonawa rete iru. Kōshita torikumi ni tsuite kondō kōji wa `gēmu ga hoka no media to chigau no wa, riarutaimu ni han'nō ga aru to iu koto. Kono intarakutibu-sei o ikashita saundo-tsukuri o suru, saundo no aidea o morikomu koto ga ichiban jūyōda to kangaete iru' to katatte iru [34]. Tsūshin zerudanodensetsu shirīzu wa kihontekini hitori de asobu sakuhin ga ōiga,“toraifōsu 3 jūshi” “4tsu no ken” nado wa maruchipurei o zentei to shita shisutemu to natte iru [35]. Sono hoka “fushigi no konomi” no rinku shisutemu [36],“4tsu no ken +” no konekutibiti (gēmukyūbu to gēmubōiadobansu no tsūshin) [37],“mugen no sunadokei” no nintendō waifai konekushon ni taiō shita tsūshin taisen ya surechigaitsūshin [38] nado samazamana katachi de tsūshin yōso ga toriire rarete iru. Zerudarashi-sa zerudanodensetsu shirīzu no kaihatsu-sha intabyū nado de hinshutsu suru mongon de [39], ōmune shodai kara uketsuga reru shirīzu no dentō shisutemu ya kūki-kan o sasuga, gengo-ka sa rete iru wakede wanaku sono teigi ni tsuite wa onōno no kankei-sha de kangaekata ga chigatte iru. Rei to shite “towairaitopurinsesu” hatsubai-ji no intabyū de miyamoto shigeru wa `ogyakusan o bakanishite nai koto'da to shite iru [40]. Nintendō moto shachō no iwatasatoru wa `hito no kazu dake zerudarashi-sa no teigi wa ari, sore ga sukoshizutsu kasanariai saishūtekini matomaru. Sarani ieba gengo-ka sa rete inai teigi janai koto koso ga zeruda ga yutakadearu riyū' to nobete iru [41]. Mata, purode~yūsā no Aonuma ni yoru to zeruda wa saishūtekini wa Miyamoto ga purode~yūsu o okonau zushiki ni natte ori, sasaina enshutsu nado ni `Miyamoto chekku (tsūshō chabudai-gaeshi)' to iu kakunin sagyō ga sōnyū sa reru to shite iru. Chekku suru bubun wa gēmu ni totte sasaide wa aru ga, sorera no tsumikasane de `zerudarashi-sa' wa kōchiku sa rete iru to iu [12]. Zeruda no atarimae 2013-nen 1 tsuki 23-nichi ni hōei sa reta `Nintendo dairekuto' no naka de, purode~yūsā no aonuma eiji ni yori, kongo no zerudashirīzu ni tsuite `zeruda no atarimae o minaosu'(Ei: Rethink the conventions of Zelda) to iu kaihatsu hōshin ga shimesa reta. Eizōde wa, minaosubeki `zeruda no atarimae' no rei to shite `shinario ni sotte susumeru'`junban ni danjon o kōryaku suru'`hitori de mokumokuto asobu' no 3ttsu ga age rareta. Torikumi no ikkan to shite, 2013-nen hatsubai no “kamigami no toraifōsu 2”de wa monogatari kōhan no danjon o [23] [20], 2017-nen hatsubai no “buresuobuzawairudo”de wa hobo subete no danjon o junfudō de kōryaku dekiru shisutemu ni natte iru. Hyōka uriage uriage zerudanodensetsu shirīzu no sekai ruikei uriage honsū wa 2023-nen jiten de 1 oku 3, 000 man-pon ni tasshite iru [42]. Hyōka gēmu naiyō no hyōka to shite “tokinookarina” “kazenotakuto” “sukaiu~ōdosōdo” “buresuobuzawairudo” wa shūkan famiutsu kurosurebyū de manten no 40-ten o kakutoku shite iru hoka [43], nintendō 3 DS to u~ī Ude wa 2015-nen 2 tsuki jiten de “tokinookarina 3 D” “mujuranokamen 3 D” “kamigami no toraifōsu 2” “kazenotakuto HD” ga Metacritic ni oite metasukoa 85 ijō yūzāsukoa 8. 5 Ijō to iu hyōka o ete iru [44]. Mata,“sukaiu~ōdosōdo” wa kaigai no karakuchi media ni mo saikō kessaku to no hyōka ga e rareta to iu [45]. Ippō de purode~yūsā no aonuma eiji ni yoreba negatibuna hyōka mo sukunakunaku,“tokinookarina” kara egara o ōkiku kaeta “kazenotakuto”de wa e no sukikirai de hyōka ga wakareta to furikaette iru [46]. Shikashi jikangatatsu to sono negatibuna iken o minaoshi hyōka ga takamatte iku keikō ga aru to ī, Nintendo of amerika (Nintendō no Beikoku hōjin) no biru torinen (eigo-ban) wa kore o `zeruda saikuru' to yonde iru [26]. Taitoru rogo nipponban no taitoru rogo wa, shodai “zerudanodensetsu” kara “bīesuzerudanodensetsu kodai no sekiban” made ( “rinkunobōken” o nozoku),`zerudanodensetsu' no moji o katadotta hobo onaji rogo taipu ga mochii rarete ita. Nihon kokugai-bande wa,“The Legend of Zelda: A rinku to the Past” ( “kamigami no toraifōsu” ) no sai ni `Z ' no moji ga ōkime ni egaka reta rogo taipu ga saiyō sa re, ikō, kono dezain ga saishin no sakuhin made hikitsuga rete iru. Nipponban “tokinookarina”de wa tōsho, jūrai to dōyō no rogo taipu o seisaku shite itaga [yō shutten], saishūtekini Nihon kokugai-ban to onaji rogo ga saiyō sa reta. Kore ikō,“towairaitopurinsesu HD” made zen sekai de hobo kyōtsū no rogo ga mochii rareru yō ni natta ( “yume o miru shima DX” o nozoku). “Mujuranokamen” ikō no sakuhinde wa, rogo no mawari ya haikei ni sakuhin o shōchō suru irasuto ga haisa rete iru. “Kazenotakuto” nadode wa “tokinookarina” izen no `zerudanodensetsu' no rogo taipu o chīsaku heiki shite iru. Ippō de “towairaitopurinsesu” nadode wa nihongo taitoru no hyōki ga naku, Nihon kokugai-ban no rogo taipu to mattaku onaji mono ni natte iru. “Buresu obu za wairudo” ikō no nipponban sakuhin no ichibude wa shodai no rogo taipu o tōshū shita dezain ni kaiki shi,`zerudanodensetsu' no moji no shita ni Ei dai ga chīsame ni heiki sa rete iru. Mata `zerudanodensetsu' no moji o katadotta rogo taipu ga mochii rareta sakuhin no uchi, shodai “zerudanodensetsu” to, shodai no rogo taipu o tōshū to shita “buresu obu za wairudo” to “tiāzu Obu za kingudamu”, orijinaru no rimeikudearu Nintendo Switch-ban no “yume o miru shima” nadode wa `da' no kabu-bun ga nobite iru. Kyarakutā※ dōmei kyarakutā ga fukusū sakuhin ni tōjō shite iruga, ichibu o nozoki dōitsujinbutsude wa naku, sakuhin-goto ni settei ga kotonaru. Rinku shujinkō [6 ]. Midori no fuku to sankaku bōshi o mi ni tsuketa hairaru no yūsha [47]. Samazamana aitemu o kushi shite nazo o toki, kyoaku to tatakau. To~ūnrinku iwayuru nekomoku de, komikku-chō no egara no rinku [48]. “Kazenotakuto” ga hatsu tōjō de,-igo keitai gēmu-kide wa omoni kono rinku ga saiyō sa rete iru [49]. Zeruda omoni hairaruōkoku no ōjo to shite tōjō. Sakuhin ni yotte settei wa chigauga, monogatari no jūyōna yakuwari o ninau koto ga ōi [47]. Sono sekai no heiwa no shōchō no yōna sonzai de, sakuhin ni yotte wa rinku to tomoni bōken suru mono mo aru [6 ]. Ganondorofu “tokinookarina” “kazenotakuto” “towairaitopurinsesu” “tiāzuobuzakingudamu” ni tōjō suru dai maō.
